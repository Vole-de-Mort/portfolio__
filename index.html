What is a club

In a high school context, a club is a student organization that brings together students with shared interests or goals. High school clubs are typically extracurricular activities, meaning they are not part of the formal academic curriculum but are offered outside of regular school hours. High school clubs
cover a wide range of interests and activities, catering to diverse
student passions. learn more

Benefits of clubs

Joining a club in high school can be a great way for students to explore
their interests, develop new skills, meet like-minded peers, and contribute
to the school community. Clubs often organize events, competitions, fundraisers, and community service projects, providing students with opportunities for personal growth and meaningful experiences beyond their regular academic studies. learn more
